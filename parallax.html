Argil DX is new age Digital Experience company. It is amalgamation of ZAP Technology Solutions LLC (US) and Accunity Software Pvt Ltd (India). Both companies bring in their unique qualities such as fresh thinking, digital transformation expertise, tremendous experience, knack to innovate and a common vision to delivery quality experience for the clients. We focus on the future while living in the present and being completely aware of the past. That drives our thinking, impact and presence.
